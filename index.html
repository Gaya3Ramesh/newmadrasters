What's in store for you?

In this fast-paced technological world, focusing on design that resonates with everyone is no longer a choice but a necessity.

This is why inclusive design is a big deal. Inclusive design provides you with opportunities to understand human perspectives, abilities, and backgrounds to create products that make everyone feel included.

One user is not the same as another. Designing one-size-fits-all isn't the best approach to creating a positive user experience. What do we do, then?

Bring inclusive design into the picture. Inclusive design defies the concept of designing one thing for everyone. It helps you shift your focus towards mindful and diverse design possibilities that offer your audience a sense of belonging.

Don't know where to start on inclusive design? We've got you covered!

Join us as our design experts decode the nuances of inclusive design for you. Sign up now to connect with like-minded peers who also think inclusive design is the need of the hour!

Speakers

Sarun Ramachandran

Product Designer at Fi

With over 6 years of experience in product design, Sarun has in-depth expertise in creating B2C digital products. His passion for inclusive design skyrocketed during his Master's program in Universal Design at the National Institute of Design. He acquired valuable, hands-on insights into the inclusive design process and its benefits there.

Currently, Sarun is a part of the design team at Fi, a modern finance management app that simplifies financial matters for professionals. Before joining Fi, he worked at Obvious, a strategic design consultancy, where he worked with prestigious clients like Kotak, Slice, and Grab.

Akash S

Lead Product Designer at Tekion Corp

An experienced UX professional with nearly a decade of hands-on industry experience, Akash is passionate about designing cohesive solutions for intricate workflows. Before becoming a part of Tekion, he has also worked with companies like Citrix and Motorola Solutions.

His extensive background encompasses the development of enterprise solutions in healthcare, public safety, virtualization, and accounting. Beyond his professional life, Akash is a mentor, chess enthusiast, and an avid traveler, constantly seeking inspiration from diverse cultures.
